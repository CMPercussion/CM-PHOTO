About Me

Hi, I'm Caleb Mooneyham — a portrait and wedding photographer based in Pontotoc, Mississippi, serving families and couples all across North Mississippi.

I believe the best photos happen when people forget the camera's even there. That's why you won't find a lot of stiff posing or "chin down, shoulders back" direction in my sessions. Instead, I'll give you a little guidance to get things started, then let the moment take over — the real laugh, the inside joke, the way you actually hold your kid's hand. Those are the details I'm after.

My goal for every session is simple: make it feel easy. Easy to show up as yourselves, easy to relax in front of the camera, and easy to work with me from our first conversation to the moment your gallery lands in your inbox. I want you looking back at these photos years from now and remembering how that day actually felt — not just how it looked.

If that sounds like the kind of experience you're looking for, I'd love to hear from you.
